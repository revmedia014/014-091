Brightening Meals with Citrus

Citrus fruits like lemons, limes, and oranges add brightness and acidity to dishes, enhancing their flavors and making them more vibrant. A squeeze of lemon can elevate a simple salad, while orange zest adds a refreshing twist to desserts. Citrus fruits balance rich, heavy flavors and bring a refreshing contrast to otherwise hearty meals.


Beyond flavor, citrus fruits are packed with vitamins and antioxidants, supporting your immune system and overall health. Their tartness helps stimulate the appetite and aids in digestion. Incorporating citrus into your cooking adds a fresh and lively touch, while the natural acidity helps to brighten and balance a variety of dishes.
